PMO Governance & 36-Month Timeline

Corporate Safeguards & Execution Phases

07 / 07

Mitigating the Founder SPOF

To ensure operations run smoothly during the Chairman's high-profile regional travel, we decouple daily operations. The CEO and managing directors execute standard client tracks, while the Chairman coordinates top-of-funnel sovereign matchmaking and sports negotiations.

Information Security & Integrity

All corporate evaluations and matchmade data are protected under strict ADGM/DIFC multi-lateral NDAs, isolating competing client portfolios with separate legal channels.

36-Month Phased Growth Roadmap

  • Phase A (Months 1–6) - Market Activation & Regulatory Setup: Deploy **AGE Launch™** to establish local entities (ADGM/DIFC), manage compliance logs, and initiate growth maturity diagnostics.
  • Phase B (Months 6–18) - Pipeline Matchmaking & Commercial Scale: Activate **AGE Connect™** to bridge client products directly into regional enterprise nodes and negotiate high-value sponsorships.
  • Phase C (Months 18–36) - Joint Venture Acceleration & Capitalization: Orchestrate **AGE Scale™** to secure joint-venture structures, expand local holdings, and prepare for institutional capital raises.
