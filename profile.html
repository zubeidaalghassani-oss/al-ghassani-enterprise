Yahya Al Ghassani

Founder & Chairman | AGE – Al Ghassani Enterprises

Yahya Al Ghassani is the Founder and Chairman of AGE – Al Ghassani Enterprises, an elite strategic growth platform dedicated to helping organizations navigate cross-border expansion, unlock sovereign pathways, and secure high-value commercial alignments across the GCC.

Drawing on years of leadership, discipline, and experience operating in high-performance environments, Yahya established AGE with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between ambitious businesses and the opportunities they need to grow. His philosophy is rooted in the belief that sustainable success is built on trust, strong partnerships, and flawless execution.

Recognized for his distinguished football career, Yahya developed a reputation for vision, teamwork, and leadership under pressure. Those same principles now guide AGE's approach to business—connecting organizations with the right partners, investors, institutions, and decision-makers to accelerate growth and create long-term value.

As Founder and Chairman, Yahya focuses on strategic relationships, executive engagement, and long-term corporate vision. He works closely with business leaders, government stakeholders, family businesses, and international organizations seeking to establish or expand their presence within the GCC.

Under his leadership, AGE has been designed as more than an advisory firm. It is an enterprise built to help organizations navigate regional markets, unlock strategic opportunities, and execute growth initiatives with professionalism, confidentiality, and integrity.
